How it works

For a fixed-rate loan, your payment is the same each month. Early payments contain more interest; later payments contain more principal. To see the month-by-month breakdown, use the amortization schedule tool.

What if APR is 0%?

Then the payment is simply principal ÷ number of payments.

Does this include fees?

No. Origination fees, closing costs, and insurance are not included.

Is this financial advice?

No—this is educational. Verify loan terms with your lender.

FAQ

Quick notes about estimates and why lenders can differ.

Why might my lender’s payment be different?

Some lenders include fees, escrow (tax/insurance), rounding rules, or different interest timing. This tool is a clean estimate.
