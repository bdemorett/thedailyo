How is self-employment tax calculated?

First, net profit is multiplied by 92.35% to estimate net earnings subject to SE tax. Then the Social Security part is taxed at 12.4% up to the wage base, and Medicare is taxed at 2.9%.

What is the half-SE-tax deduction?

You can usually deduct half of your self-employment tax as an adjustment to income. This does not reduce SE tax itself, but it may reduce income tax.

Does this include Additional Medicare Tax?

No. This calculator keeps the estimate simple and does not add the high-income Additional Medicare Tax.
