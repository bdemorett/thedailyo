How it works

This converter uses the exact international conversion factor between miles and kilometers. It is useful for travel, running, driving distances, maps, and international unit conversion.

Miles to kilometers formula
kilometers = miles × 1.609344
Kilometers to miles formula
miles = kilometers ÷ 1.609344
How many kilometers are in 1 mile?

Exactly 1.609344 kilometers.

Why do some results look rounded?

The converter uses the exact factor, but the displayed result is rounded to the number of decimal places you choose.

Can I use this for race distances?

Yes. It works well for 5K, 10K, half marathon, marathon, and other training or event conversions.
