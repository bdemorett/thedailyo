Start here

Most career income questions come down to three things: What’s my hourly rate?, What’s my annual salary?, and What does a raise actually change? Start with salary conversions, then model raises.

Tip: When comparing job offers, use the same assumptions every time: hours per week, weeks per year, and whether unpaid time off is included.
