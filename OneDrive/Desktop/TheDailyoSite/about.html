What is TheDailyo?
TheDailyo is a growing set of simple calculators and tools that help you answer common, real-life questions quickly: “What’s my hourly rate if I make $70,000?”, “How long until my credit card is paid off?”, “How many days until a date?”.

Each tool is designed to be easy to use, with plain-English explanations so the numbers make sense.
What kinds of tools are here?
Work & Salary
Salary ↔ hourly, overtime math, raise planning, paycheck estimates.
Personal Finance
Payoff timelines, interest growth, budgeting basics, retirement estimates.
Time & Dates
Days until, weeks between dates, age calculations, planning timelines.
Health
Simple calculators like BMI and other everyday fitness math.
Business
Markup vs margin, break-even, sales tax, and small-business basics.
Accuracy & assumptions
We use standard math and common definitions. When a calculator depends on assumptions (like workdays per year, compounding frequency, or schedule), the tool lets you change inputs so the results match your situation.
