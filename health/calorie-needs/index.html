FAQ

What formula does this use?

It uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to estimate BMR (baseline calories), then multiplies by an activity factor to estimate TDEE.

Why doesn’t this perfectly match my results?

Real calorie needs vary by body composition, sleep, stress, job movement, and tracking accuracy. Use this as a starting point and adjust based on 2–4 weeks of trends.

Can I use this for teens?

It’s designed for adults. For children/teens, calorie needs can differ due to growth and should be handled with professional guidance.
