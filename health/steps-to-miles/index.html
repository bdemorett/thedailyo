FAQ

How does the conversion work?

Distance = steps × step length. Then we convert meters to miles/kilometers.

What step length should I use?

If you can measure your stride (custom), use that. Otherwise, the “average” option is a reasonable starting point.

Why estimate step length from height?

Height correlates with stride length. It can be closer than a generic “average,” but still isn’t perfect.
