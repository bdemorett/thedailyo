Section Item Value Notes

How to interpret the results

“Hourly (paid hours)” spreads your total estimated annual pay across paid hours. “Effective hourly” can subtract PTO hours from worked hours (if checked), which can make jobs with more PTO look better—even at the same salary.

What should I set for paid days off?

Include PTO + paid holidays. Many employers are around 10–30 days total.

Is overtime always paid on top of salary?

No. This is just a scenario tool. Some roles are exempt and do not pay OT.
